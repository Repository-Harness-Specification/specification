# Code of Conduct

We want this project to be a productive place for technical disagreement and collaboration.

Participants are expected to:

- communicate respectfully;
- criticize ideas rather than people;
- provide evidence when making technical claims;
- disclose important conflicts of interest;
- avoid harassment, discrimination, threats, and personal attacks.

Maintainers may remove content or restrict participation when behavior repeatedly prevents constructive collaboration.

Report serious conduct concerns privately to the maintainers through the contact method listed in the organization profile.
